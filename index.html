The Companies
Two Platforms.
One Mission.

ISK Therapeutics

Immune System Key Ltd. (est. 2005, Jerusalem) developed a proprietary AI-driven proteogenomics platform that screens the entire human genome to discover novel therapeutic molecules. Using Fuzzy Dynamics, evolutionary computation, and macromolecular modeling, the platform identified NEROFE-101 — a natural 84-amino-acid hormone encoded in chromosome 10 — along with 20+ additional molecules. Currently in Phase 1b/2a clinical trials for AML and KRAS-mutant solid tumors with 60% response rate.

Lead: NEROFE-101 • Phase 1b/2a • AML + KRAS

TwoToBiotech

(est. 2007, subsidiary) discovered APC1 — a breakthrough molecule that transforms untreatable Triple-Negative Breast Cancer (TNBC) cells to express HER2 receptors, making them responsive to existing anti-HER2 therapies. Orphan Drug designation filed with both FDA and EMEA. A world-expert oncologist from MSKCC is collaborating on a novel drug for GI tract tumors. 8+ novel peptide hormones discovered.

The Discovery Engine

Ordinary AI.

Most companies that claim “AI drug discovery” use off-the-shelf machine learning to screen existing compound libraries faster. ISK does something fundamentally different.

Prof. Uziel Sandler — a theoretical physicist who spent decades developing the mathematics of complex systems — built a proprietary computational framework long before the world knew what AI was. His work on Fuzzy Dynamics began in the early 1990s. His publications on evolutionary computation and neural modeling predate modern deep learning by two decades.

This is not a startup that bolted a ChatGPT-style model onto a chemistry database. This is a mathematical framework purpose-built by one of the world’s leading experts in computational dynamics of biological systems.

The Algorithmic Stack
Fuzzy Dynamics

A mathematical discipline Sandler co-founded — describes evolution of complex biological systems under uncertainty. Applied to immune cell maturation and neural cell behavior since the 1990s.

S-Lagrangian Dynamics

A generalized Lagrangian theory of homeostasis that bridges theoretical physics and living systems. Published in Physica A and applied to molecular prediction.

Proteogenomic Screening

The platform scans the entire human genome for statistical anomalies in non-coding DNA that predict functional peptides invisible to conventional methods.

The Result

NEROFE was mathematically predicted before it was biologically discovered. The algorithm identified a hidden hormone in chromosome 10 — later confirmed in the thymus gland with powerful anti-cancer properties. 20+ additional molecules have since been identified by the same platform.
